About Us

Alfarad is a company working in the field of radiology since 1958 and has a previous business in various fields and various sectors, whether governmental or private.

We supply stationery, provide information technology services and solutions, electronics, supply general consumables, commercial printing services and general supplies, medical supplies, clinic equipment* servers and special programs, printing and imaging papers, printers and photocopiers, inks of various kinds, medical devices (X-rays and analysis), outdoor and indoor advertising, computers and networks, surveillance cameras.

We focus on offering our customers quality at the most affordable prices. Our goal is to deliver beyond our customers' expectations. We have a highly committed team ready to work and partner with you, helping you achieve your organization's goal effectively and efficiently.

Our Products and Services

Teleradiology

The company includes a distinguished group of consu Itants and specialists specialized in all subspecialties of radiology and its various branches. The company also provides technical support (PACS) for contracted centers and hospitals with technical support service 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Printers

The company provides high-quality and efficient printers and all printing supplies, including original inks and paper of various sizes, in addition to technical support 24 hours a day 7 days a week, at special competitive prices.

Equipping Specialized Radiology Centers

The company carries out all construction works related to radiology rooms including electrical works, shoring, and optimal design of equipment rooms. The company also completes all legal procedures to equip radiology

Radiology Centers Management

The company organizes work in radiology centers, starting from various appointments (receptionists - technicians - nursing - specialists). The company also sets work systems within the center and trains different teams on professional and optimal work methods through the management of different radiology centers.
